    The Tiger

    •The tiger is the biggestmember of the cat family.It is only rivaled by lions in strength.A tiger has dark vertical stripes on its orange-brown fur.Furthermore,it is an apex predator.

    Different types of tigers exist namely:

    • Bengal tiger
    • Siberian tiger
    • Sumatran tiger
    • White tiger

    Bengal tiger

    •Native to the Indian subcontinent.The subspecies has the largest population in the world.Bengal tigers are incredibly strong-they are able to drag prey almost twice their size.

    Siberian tiger

    Largest subspecies and largest cat in the world.The Siberian tiger resides in the southeast region of Russia,China and NorthKorea.They are known to be solitary animals.Since they live in regions of cold climate,they developed physical adaptations.For example,they developed a layer of fat and a denser furto protect them from extreme cold temperatures.

    Sumatran tiger

    The Sumatran tiger resides only in the Indonesian island of Sumatra.They are physically the smallest subspecies of the tigers.Their population is also the smallest(around 600)

    White tiger

    Also known asbleached tiger.Has a white coat with black stripes.The white tiger lacks a pigment called pheomelanin,which is responsible for the orange colour found in Bengal tigers.The white tiger lives in some parts of Indian states.
